OUR STORY

Welcome to StitchMaster!

StitchMaster is a quaint little store in the heart of Omaha's shopping hub housing a refined amalgamation of traditional techniques, rich textiles and progressive silhouettes – true traditions that weave into ethnic contemporary. At StitchMaster, we work with different textile designers, design studios and artisans across United States who have a passion for their craft and for the rich history of our country, which directly translates into their products. Thank you for stopping by to know more about us; If you’d like to touch the fabrics, feel the love in the products or just chat, come meet us at our store!
